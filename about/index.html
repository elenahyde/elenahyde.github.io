About

Elena Hyde is an artist from the North Shore of Oahu.  She has grown up loving art from a very young age.  She is attending Brigham Young University - Hawaii and is working on her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Painting and Illustration.  Her love of art and creation has lead her to try many different mediums.  From watercolor, oil paint, ceramics, pen and ink, anything that she can get her hands on is where she loves to be.  Her work often portrays themes and ideas of storytelling from around the world, exploration of color, and animals. Her work has been exhibited at Brigham Young University - Hawaii.
